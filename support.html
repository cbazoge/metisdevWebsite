exhala - Breathing Exercise App

exhala is designed to guide you through science-backed breathing exercises to help reduce stress, improve focus, and promote relaxation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a breathing session?

Open the app, browse the available exercises, and tap on one to begin. Follow the visual guide to breathe in sync with the animation.

Can I track my progress?

Yes. The app tracks your sessions, streaks, and total practice time. View your history in the Settings screen.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

The app isn't working correctly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure you have the latest version installed. If problems continue, contact us at support@metisdev.com with details about the issue.
