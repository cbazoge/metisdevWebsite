About Metis Dev

We believe great software should be invisible—it should feel natural, intuitive, and simply work.

Metis Dev is a software studio focused on creating polished, user-centered applications. Named after the Greek goddess of wisdom and skill, we approach each project with careful thought and craftsmanship.

Our philosophy is simple: build fewer things, but build them well. Every feature, every interaction, every pixel is considered with intention.
